DEMO CALL - GUARDIAN ALARM SYSTEMS - CASE 005

Clara Team: Hello, thanks for your time. Tell us about your business.

Client: I'm Patricia Nguyen, owner of Guardian Alarm Systems in Atlanta Georgia.
We install and service fire alarms, security alarms, and access control systems.
15 years in business. 12 technicians. We use ServiceTrade.
Hours are Monday to Friday 8am to 5pm Eastern Time.

Clara Team: What is an emergency for your business?

Client: Fire alarm activation at a client site, alarm system complete failure,
and access control failures at hospitals or schools. Those are our top three.

Clara Team: How do emergencies get routed?

Client: Emergency calls go to our 24 hour monitoring center first at 404-555-0561.
They assess and dispatch. If it is a true emergency they call our on-call tech.
We rotate on-call weekly. The current on-call number changes every Monday.

Clara Team: Any constraints we should know?

Client: Never promise same day service unless it is a confirmed emergency.
Never create alarm inspection jobs in ServiceTrade without a signed work order first.

Clara Team: Call volume?

Client: About 35 to 55 calls per week.
