Clara Team: What happens if Mike doesn't answer?

Client: Then it goes to our backup dispatcher Sarah at 720-555-0192.

Clara Team: Any jobs you don't take?

We also never create sprinkler jobs directly in ServiceTrade without supervisor approval.

Clara Team: How many calls roughly per week?
