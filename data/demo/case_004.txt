DEMO CALL - BRIGHT SPARK ELECTRICAL - CASE 004

Clara Team: Good afternoon. Tell us about your company.

Client: Hi I am David Okafor, owner of Bright Spark Electrical in Houston Texas.
Commercial electrical contractor. 10 years in business. About 20 electricians on staff.
We use ServiceTitan as our CRM. Business hours Monday to Friday 8am to 5pm Central Time.

Clara Team: What are your emergency types?

Client: Power outages at commercial properties, electrical fires or sparks, 
generator failures, and any situation where a building has lost power completely.

Clara Team: Emergency routing?

Client: All emergencies go to our 24 hour dispatch line 713-555-0451.
If dispatch doesn't answer in 60 seconds, it should escalate to our emergency 
supervisor Kevin at 713-555-0452. We always want a human to answer emergencies, never voicemail.

Clara Team: What don't you do?

Client: No residential work at all. No solar panel installation.
Never book jobs under $500 without manager approval.

Clara Team: Call volume?

Client: 50 to 80 calls a week. Lot of repeat commercial clients.
